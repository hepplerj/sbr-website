---
title: "Between the Fences"
description: "A digital history of the sagebrush rebellions — recurring fights over land, power, and federal authority in the American West from the 1930s through 2016."
---

Federal authority over the public lands of the American West has been contested again and again — in the grazing politics of the 1930s, the Sagebrush Rebellion of 1979, the Wise Use movement of the 1990s, and the armed standoffs at Bundy Ranch in 2014 and Malheur in 2016. This project treats these episodes as a single long history rather than separate incidents: a recurring American political form, shaped by a specific geography, a specific set of federal institutions, and a specific set of coalitions.

The work is organized into four entry points. Each is readable on its own; together they make the argument.
