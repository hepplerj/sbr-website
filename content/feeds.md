*Governing Ground* publishes two RSS feeds. Both are auto-discoverable, so most browsers and feed readers will pick them up from any page on the site, but you can subscribe directly using the URLs below.

## Writing

<https://governingground.org/narrative/index.xml>

The **primary feed.** Subscribe here to be notified when a new essay is published in the [Writing](/narrative/) section. Long-form work — narrative chapters, research notes, and occasional digressions — appears here a few times a year as the project develops.

## Changelog

<https://governingground.org/updates/index.xml>

The **changelog feed.** Subscribe here to follow incremental changes: new visualizations, revised data, restructured sections, corrections. The [Changelog](/updates/) section reads like a project diary; entries appear more often than essays do.

## Other sections

The Sightlines, Bibliography, Data, and About sections do not publish RSS. New visualizations and data sources are announced through the changelog feed or mentioned in narratives when they go live. You can also follow me on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/jasonheppler.org) or [Micro.blog](https://updates.jasonheppler.org/) to learn about updates to the site.
