First public release of *Governing Ground*. The site launches with:

- Two narrative essays: an introduction and the Malheur chapter
- Eleven interactive sightlines (maps, network graphs, and climate charts)
- A cosponsorship network for public-lands legislation in the 108th Congress and forward
- Climate stripes for CONUS and four sub-regions (temperature and precipitation)
- Farm bankruptcy data from USDA and U.S. Courts records
- A full bibliography drawn from the project's BibTeX sources

The project will expand with additional narrative chapters and visualizations as research continues.
